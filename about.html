According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Kajla village is 547758. Kajla village is located in Badnapur tehsil of Jalna district in Maharashtra, India. It is situated 23km away from sub-district headquarter Badnapur (tehsildar office) and 16km away from district headquarter Jalna. As per 2009 stats, Kajala is the gram panchayat of Kajla village. The total geographical area of village is 1564 hectares. Kajla has a total population of 3,210 peoples, out of which male population is 1,646 while female population is 1,564. There are about 626 houses in kajla village. Jalna is nearest town to kajla for all major economic activities, which is approximately 16km away.
